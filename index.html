Public Choice Schools in Hartford, CT

The following section will begin to discuss Public choice schools in the Hartford area. These schools include magnet schools, charter schools, and technical schools.

The topic that we looked into was to chart the locations of all public choice or districts and their student population, then we did the same for all traditional public schools in Hartford and the previously agreed upon surrounding areas. Two of our most important findings are immediately visible: that the majority of public choice schools in the Hartford area are magnet schools, and they are highly concentrated in and around the city of Hartford itself. When we then looked at the map of all traditional public schools in the Hartford area we see that they are much more evenly spread out.

In figure 1 we see a map of all public choice schools in Hartford and the surrounding area. Each point represents one school. The points vary in size depending on each school’s student population. Using the live link below the map, you can zoom in to make the overlapping points more clear and scroll over each point to see more information about each school.

Quickly Edit and View Changes on Your Local Computer

HINT: Instead of slowly making edits on GitHub, you can quickly make edits in your local computer and view them instantly in your browser. Here's a list of steps (in HTML format):

  1. Go to your GitHub repo and select Code > Download Zip and uncompress the folder.
  2. Install a free code editor tool. My fav is Atom Editor but there are others, too.
  3. Open the "index.html" file with your code editor and make edits.
  4. In your browser, go to File > Open to view the "index.html" file that is on your local computer.
  5. After making some edits in the "index.html" file, save it, and refresh your browser to view changes. Most edits should appear right away.
  6. After making multiple edits on your local computer, carefully copy and paste the contents of "index.html" in your online GitHub account, and test your published updates online.
